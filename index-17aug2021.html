Deepak Raina


Prime Minister's Research Fellow (PMRF) | Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Delhi
Email: deepak.raina@mech.iitd.ac.in, raina.1@iitj.ac.in

I am currently a Ph.D. candidate under Prime Minister's Research Fellow (PMRF) scheme at IIT Delhi advised by Prof. Subir Kumar Saha. My research is centered around Robotics and Artificial Intelligence, addressing topics such as Robot Learning from Demonstration (LfD), physical human-robot interaction (pHRI) and tele-operation of robots. This research is aimed towards intelligent and physically consistent robots that interact seamlessly with humans. I am passionate about developing autonomous systems for use in medicine, particularly for the diagnosis of patients and precise surgery. I am also a student volunteer at I-Hub Foundation for Cobotics (IHFC), a non-profit (section-8) company of IIT Delhi, to develop products/solutions in the area of collaborative robotics.

Previously, I was a researcher at TCS Robotics Research and Innovation Lab (Delhi), contributing towards the automation of warehouse industry under the supervision of Dr. Swagat Kumar and Mr. Rajesh Sinha. I obtained M.Tech at IIT Jodhpur advised by Prof. Suril V. Shah. My masters research focused on space robotics, addressing autonomous on-orbit servicing (OOS) operations such as capture of debris and servicing of satellite using a multi-arm robot.
